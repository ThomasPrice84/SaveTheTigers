About Us

Save the Tigers website is a really cool project that Thomas Price and Jeffrey Wan have worked on. This website is pushing for more attention to the cause to preserve and protect the world’s tiger population. Seeing as the world’s tiger population has struggled to rebound in recent years, this project will highlight tiger data and possible points of action.
